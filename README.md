           Retail Sales & Customer Performance Dashboard 

         Project Overview  
Used Excel to understand how sales performance and customer behavior would impact business decisions.
The goal was to revenue pattern, customer segments, and product Category and subcategory(Pivot Table) performance into something easy to understand.

Data Cleaning
Using Excel for cleaning and visualization,
Using Power Query in excel to trim,clean messy data.

  Questions Business Would asked
- Which customer segment generates the most revenue?  
- How are sales performing over time?  
- Who are our top customers?  
- Which regions contribute the most to revenue?
- Which Product Category drive sales the more  

 Dashboard Breakdown  

1. KPI Section  
- Total Revenue: Overall business performance  
- Total Units Sold: Sales volume  
- Total Profit: Business profitability  
- Total Customers: Customer base size  

2. Monthly Sales Trend  
This shows how revenue changes across months,which helps ti identify whether business is increasing or decreasing.

3. Sales by Customer Segment  
This Breaks down which segment or group (Consumer,Corporate,Home Office) generate more revenue for business 

4. Top 5 Customers  
This Highlights our most valuable customers  thats contributing highly to our  revenue.

5. Regional Performance  
Thus shows which region drives more sales or produce or generate more revenue (East, West, Central, South).

      Key Insights  
- A large portion of revenue comes from our Consumer segment  
- Sales fluctuate, thats an increase an sales across months, indicating possible seasonality  
- A few top customers contribute highly to our revenue growth  

     Business Value  
This dashboard helps business teams quickly identify where revenue is coming from and which customers or regions need more attention.
